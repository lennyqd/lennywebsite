About

The information on this page will show how my varied experience in computer science, videography, and free-lance modeling enables me to address problems in a unique and meticulous way. I'm enthusiastic about technology, visual storytelling, and using my work to make a difference.




Computer Scientist with a Passion for Technology

During my studies, I undertook various projects in different coding languages, gaining valuable experience in the technical world. I have a strong foundation in software development, algorithms, and problem-solving, making me adept at tackling complex challenges in the tech industry.

My passion for technology drives me to stay updated with the latest trends in the field, ensuring that I am always equipped with cutting-edge skills to create innovative solutions.


Passionate Videographer and Founder of "Capture the People"

Apart from my computer science journey, I am also a dedicated videographer, with a strong focus on capturing precious moments and creating captivating visuals.

What started as a photography and videography venture for weddings and events evolved into a full-fledged video production company for music artists called Capture the People, the idea to record the moments with the community and all around it. Capture the People gained popularity on social media, attracting numerous music artists who sought to collaborate on music video projects.

My experience in videography has allowed me to master the art of storytelling through visuals. I am committed to creating compelling videos that not only showcase the talent of the artists but also resonate with their audience, spreading their music to a wider audience.


Freelance Model with Certified Professional Model (CPM) Credential

Alongside my videography pursuits, I am also an experienced freelance model, mastering the art of posing and showcasing confidence in front of the camera.

My journey in modeling started as a means to learn more about editing skills for my photography and videography projects. As I gained more experience, I honed my posing techniques and became well-versed in presenting myself confidently and professionally.

The Certified Professional Model (CPM) certification, earned through the National Association of Professional Models (NAPM), is a testament to my dedication to success in the modeling industry. It covers essential topics such as marketing, branding, portfolio development, and business management, equipping me with the skills to excel in my modeling endeavors.
